Privacy Policy — Vibe IPTV Player

Effective date: 26 June 2026  |  Last updated: 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vibe Labs ("we", "us") handles information in the Vibe IPTV Player application (the "App"). We have designed the App to be local-first: your data stays on your device, and we do not operate any server that collects it.

Summary (the short version)

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to us.
  • We have no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking SDKs.
  • The App contains no channels or media content. You supply your own playlists.
  • All your data — playlists, provider credentials, favorites, settings, and downloads — is stored only on your device.

Information stored on your device

The App stores the following locally on your device (in its private app storage) and never sends it to us:

  • Playlists and provider details you add (M3U/M3U8 URLs, Xtream Codes server, username, and password).
  • Favorites, watch history/resume positions, and app settings/preferences.
  • Downloaded media you choose to save for offline playback.
  • Diagnostic logs generated on your device. These are kept locally and are only shared if you explicitly export/share them (for example, to send us a bug report). They may contain technical details such as stream URLs and error messages — please review a report before sharing it.

You can remove this data at any time by deleting it within the App or by uninstalling the App.

Network connections

The App connects to the internet only to perform the functions you request:

  • Your IPTV provider(s): The App connects directly to the server addresses you enter to load playlists and play streams. Your credentials are sent only to your chosen provider, not to us.
  • Channel logos and artwork: Images referenced by URLs inside your playlist are loaded directly from those URLs.
  • Local network (QR pairing): To transfer a playlist between your phone and TV, the App can run a temporary HTTP server on your local network only. This is used solely for the device-to-device transfer you initiate and is not accessible over the internet.

We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party IPTV providers or image hosts you choose to connect to.

Device permissions

  • Internet / Network state / Wi-Fi state — to load and play your streams and to perform local QR pairing.
  • Camera — used only to scan a QR code for playlist pairing. Camera images are processed on-device and are never stored or transmitted by us.
  • Notifications & Foreground service — to show playback/download status and to keep downloads running.

Optional Google Cast (Chromecast)

If your device supports Google Cast, the App can stream to a Cast device. When you use Cast, the relevant Google Cast services handle the connection; this feature is inactive on devices without Google Play Services (such as Amazon Fire TV).

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking of any kind. We do not build profiles, sell data, or share data with advertisers — because we do not collect it.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data security

Because your data stays on your device, its security depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device and operating system up to date.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and posting the revised Policy at its published URL.
